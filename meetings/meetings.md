## 29-10-2025

<img width="696" height="94" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/74310835-96a8-4848-98f9-564ebcf7ee5d" />

Overall Discussion:

- Identified problem statement
- Thought about proposed solution
- Free and Paid tiers - how to monetise

Problem:

- Sharing and finding photos is a messy, time-consuming process
- there is low incentive for contributors. The incentive model is to make users upload x number of images to unlock the full gallery
- all photos are hosted in a secure private album, with explicit consent required for facial scanning and an option to buy premium downloads
  Features:
- I upload then I’ll get pictures
- Curation (”Highlights”)
- Monetization for uploaders

Additional Features:

- Automatic photo albums for groups of friends(??)
- Event highlights, Montage or Recap reels
- Integration with 3rd party social media

**Types of user journeys:**

- Standard user
  - Example : guest at a wedding
    1. Entry : The user scans a QR code at the even
    2. Identity & consent: They are then prompted to upload a selfie for identification and gives consent for facial recognition
       1. The app will build the gallery, but they will only see low resolution photos
    3. Contribution: To see/download their high resolution photos, the user will be prompted to upload at least x number of photos from their cameral roll
    4. Premium Choice:
       1. The user can choose to pay one time fee and gain immediate high resolution access without uploading
          1. This converts a standard user into a paying customer
       2. User will have access to the photographers gallery
    5. Free tier:
       1. You will know how many pictures you’re in, before paying
       2. Can these features without a subscription

          The app can support additional features such as:
          - Automatic photo albums for groups of friends (??)
          - Event highlights, Montage or Recap reels
          - Integration with 3rd party social media
- Business user
  1. The organiser purchases a business premium to create the event gallery
     1. The main revenue for the app
  2. Photographers
     1. Will use their special access to upload their high resolution photos
  3. Monetisation:
     1. The photographer can licence photos to others (tips)
        1. Users can pay a small fee/tip to the uploader of a specific photo they like
        2. This rewards the photographer directly for their work
        3. Quick and easy payment
     2. Attendees can buy prints from the uploader
        1. work with a 3rd party printing service. When a guest orders a print, the original uploader will receive a commission on the sale
        2. We get a fee for helping the sale
  4. Boost usage:
     1. Tiered rewards: upload 100 + good photos → get 1 month premium free
        1. Gamification - it motivates the users to upload their camera roll
